Our Mission

Contribute to addressing the issue of food waste by simultaneously providing food to individuals in need.

The issue of food waste spans from agricultural production to landfill, encompassing various stages. A significant portion, exceeding 40%, of edible food prepared by restaurants worldwide remains uneaten and ultimately discarded. Our objective is to address this problem by establishing a platform that facilitates collaboration between homeless shelters, charities, and individuals willing to assist. Through this platform, they can connect with restaurants to negotiate reduced prices for surplus food, thereby minimizing waste.

Global food waste has an enormous environmental impact, too.
